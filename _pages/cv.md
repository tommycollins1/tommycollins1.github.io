Profile
======
Analytical researcher and spatial data specialist with experience designing and delivering complex intelligence and risk-analysis projects using behavioural, environmental, demographic, and geospatial datasets. Skilled in statistical modelling, GIS, machine learning, and evidence-led analysis to support strategic planning and decision-making, with a strong understanding of data governance, ethical data use, and interdisciplinary collaboration.

Current Position
======
**Postdoctoral Researcher**, Centre for Environmental Intelligence, University of Exeter *(Oct 2025 – present)*
* Designing and leading complex spatial and statistical analyses integrating behavioural, environmental, demographic, and remotely sensed datasets to support strategic environmental planning and evidence-led decision-making.
* Developing analytical frameworks to assess environmental risk, model the impacts of development scenarios on protected sites and communities, and identify future pressures relevant to long-term planning and policy.
* Producing technical reports, visualisations, methodology documents, and analytical briefings communicating complex findings to government agencies, local authorities, and interdisciplinary partners.
* Collaborating with Natural England, local planning authorities, and interdisciplinary research teams to deliver outputs aligned with national environmental policy frameworks.

Previous Experience
======
**PhD Researcher**, University of Exeter *(Oct 2021 – Oct 2025)*
* Designed advanced analytical workflows integrating mobile phone mobility data, app usage data, remote sensing, and machine learning to model urban behaviour, movement patterns, and spatial dynamics.
* Developed spatial and statistical modelling frameworks (Elastic Net, Random Forests, geographically weighted models) to identify drivers of inequality, vulnerability, and urban activity.
* Produced peer-reviewed publications, conference presentations, and technical outputs for technical, policy, and non-technical audiences.

**Research Data Officer**, Royal Cornwall Hospitals Trust *(Oct 2021 – Feb 2023)*

* Aligned data management practices with regulatory guidelines, Trust policies, and standard operating procedures under Good Clinical Practice.
* Supported research governance including ethics submissions, sponsor communications, and HRA compliance.
